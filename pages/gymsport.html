GYM

Why do you need GYM?

Regular exercise enhances physical health, reduces chronic disease risk, promotes mental health by reducing stress, and fosters a sense of community among individuals with similar goals.

Why the gym is worth it?

Joining a gym can improve mental and physical health, build muscle, and improve sleep quality, as per the Mayo Clinic's long-term exercise program.

How much gym is necessary?

To maintain a healthy gym routine, aim for 150-75 minutes of moderate aerobic activity weekly, or an equal combination of both, spread over several days.
