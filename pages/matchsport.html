SPORT

Why do you need sports?

Sports promote fitness, healthy weight management, and encourage healthy decision-making, while also lowering the risk of osteoporosis and breast cancer.

Why do sports make us happy?

Exercise boosts endorphins, dopamine, adrenaline, and endocannabinoid, brain chemicals linked to happiness, confidence, capability, reduced anxiety, stress, and physical pain.

Why is sport unique?

Sport is unique due to its irrational passions, symbolic significance, and unique performance outcomes, unlike other economic and social activities that focus on achievement celebration.
